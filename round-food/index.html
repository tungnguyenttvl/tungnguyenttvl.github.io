Why need Blockchain in Food Delivery ?

Blockchain technology can help on-demand food delivery businesses by providing various benefits to developers, owners, and end-users.

  • High-level security.
  • Fraud prevention.
  • Increased efficiency.
  • Speed.
  • Transparency for restaurants and customers.

About Round Food

Round Food is a business platform - a full-featured ecosystem that brings together all economic actors (sellers, distributors, buyers, banks, insurance and logistics companies, services). tax, customs, ...) into a single blockchain platform.

Why Round Food ?

Token-powered

Using blockchain technology allows us to build a more efficient food ecosystem. One where everyone helps improve and grow the network.

Entrepreneur-first

Business owners are given full control over their payments, menus, prices, orders, advertising, and analytics. They can speak directly with customers and get paid directly.

Community-driven

We grow organically. We unite restaurant owners, their loyal fans, and blockchain talent in our active Round Food Community by fairly rewarding everyone’s contributions.
