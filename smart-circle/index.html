How Smart Circle work?

Find and book your parking spot near some of the most interesting places in your country.

Step 1

List a spot

Use Smart Circle to list your available spot.

Step 2

Find a spot

Enter your destination, compare parking spots and prices near where you want to park and you’ll find a number of parking spots listed by Smart Circle parking spot owners.

Step 3

Accept a Booking

Once your parking spot is listed, you’re notified when a driver wants to book it. All that remains is your approval to rent out your parking spot.

Step 4

Book your Spot

Browse through available parking spots and choose the best one.

Step 5

Park

Navigate to your parking spot at the time of your booking and park safely and conveniently.

Step 6

Get rewarded

Smart Circle community is rewarded with tokens for good behavior.

Real Life Cases

Several cities in Taiwan have deployed over a thousand IoT sensors to detect available parking spots. All information is then collected in the “Easy Parking in Taipei” APP and can be accessed by any driver in Taiwan. It reduces traffic jams and reduces the time wasted to locate the driver's car. The solution is currently being deployed in Taipei (1000 sensors), Tainan (2000 sensors) and Pingtung (1600 sensors).

Singapore has implemented a smart parking solution with dual sensor technology in private residences to effectively manage parking spaces in real time.
