What is Dandelion ?

Dandelion is China's ad-free social media platform where many individuals can create and find useful articles. With the DAN token as the main driving force, the platform removes hidden advertising and marketing so that anyone can quickly access and find high-quality articles and trusted individuals.

The Problem

Social media is broken.
Advertising-driven social networks are taking a toll on our wellbeing.

  • Invade our privacy and sell our data

  • Have control and custody of our online identities

  • Are filled with trolling, harassment, and flame wars

  • Manipulate our feeds to increase engagement at almost any cost

Dandelion is Different

Dandelion fills the gap with insight, responsibility, and self-awareness.

  • Many high-quality articles can be accessed quickly and easily.

  • The value of the platform is passed on to users.

  • Data reliability is possible with Blockchain at a lower cost than before.

Why Blockchain ?

Blockchain puts you in control

On Dandelion, accounts and posts are stored on the blockchain. That gives you exclusive control over your account and content. No intermediaries are needed for you to post or access data.

Do not edit or delete

Dandelion is publicly accessible, possibly forever. This creates accountability and gives your words more weight.
Dandelion is strongly censorship resistant because blockchain is immutable. What is written to the blockchain cannot be edited or deleted.

Transparency

All data is stored publicly on the blockchain. Anyone can monitor and check censored content on Dandelion.

Spam resistant

Dandelion is anti-spam. Spammers lose their free peek privilege, making spammers a relatively expensive prospect.

Verification

Verify your identity by linking your other social media accounts via blockchain.

Other blockchain services

There are many other possibilities for blockchain-based services in the future. Dandelion can interact with other smart contracts to provide decentralized services.
