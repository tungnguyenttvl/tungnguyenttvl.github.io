The concept behind this easy blockchain solution is to solve common problems in the fashion industry, enhancing the overall customer experience.

Overview Solist Show

Solist Show is a blockchain-based decentralized network-based platform that makes it possible for consumers to access product history and proof of authenticity of fashion goods - from sourcing to sales.

Solist Show offers a complete solution by bringing the fashion industry, blockchain technology and RFID/NFC together, allowing blockchain technology to be accessed by everyone regardless of size while maintaining the flexibility to address the specific needs of each industry.

Benefits of blockchain in fashion Solist Show

Solist Show provides retailers, manufacturers and end users with unmatched transparency, traceability, and transactional capabilities in retail fashion and supply chain management.

Transparency

This enables companies to share verifiable claims with their consumers to improve authenticity and enhance consumer trust.

Traceability

This enables companies to track the entire provenance of their products and their underlying elements from source-to-sale.

Tradability

This enables customers to tokenize non-fungible assets that can be held, purchased, exchanged and traded at any time, anywhere.

Governance Solist Show

Enhancing transparency and trust for consumers across the entire value chain.

  • Production
    Creation of a UNIQUE DIGITAL ID for each product to allow traceability during the entire product lifecycle.

  • Distribution
    RECORD OF EVERY TRANSACTION in the product’s certificate.

  • Purchase
    Delivery of an AUTHENTICITY CERTIFICATE guaranteeing the product is genuine and giving access to information about its production.

  • Care
    Keep track of the PRODUCT INTERVENTIONS (Maintenance, repairs…)

  • Reselling
    Guarantee of the product authenticity with the certificate and platform label & development of algorithm to DETECT COUNTERFEIT PRODUCTS.
