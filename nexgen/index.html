NEXGEN PROTOCOL - BLOCKCHAIN - BASED SOLUTIONS FOR THE INSURANCE INDUSTRY

NEXGEN is an autonomous insurance network - fully automated insurance for IoT devices and a platform for insurance innovation built on data. Nexgen Protocol is a new blockchain-based insurance ecosystem that seeks to connect insurers, their customers, and developers. It is designed to assist insurance companies in tailoring insurance policies to individual lifestyles to provide unique incentives for customers.

PTM helps to better predict the risk of specific events, and equips with more market knowledge thereby creating more confidence than estimates by experts or groups of individuals with the same knowledge.

Smart Contract

Smart contracts are often automated and irrevocable, underpinned by blockchain technology that can have a transformative impact on the insurance market. Smart Insurance

Smart Insurance ensures insurance policies are unique and valid. Smart Insurance provides immutable, timestamped proof of an offer and acceptance. The rules governing underwriting, implementing new policies, reviews, claims handling, repair and payment processes can all be embedded in smart contracts.

Black Box Insurance

Car insurance companies use Black Box Insurance to assess the level of risk a particular driver poses to reimburse safe drivers, provide bonus mileage allowances, or add extra money, policyholder's policy term for a lower fee. In particular, track information from APIs in sophisticated machinery and equipment such as cars, yachts, and airplanes.

Data Providers

Partnering with manufacturers whose equipment (drones, sensors, WiFi-enabled devices and vehicles, and consumables) has collected data that informs insurance work and accounting, thereby capturing new big data for many sensitive and risky markets.
