New World Platform

The New World Exchange

New World Exchange the "heart" of New World Platform, whose main function is to use a smooth interface to buy, sell and auction all kinds of game content or exchange items directly with other users.

Economy of the New World Exchange

New World Exchange has a "lifeblood" of NW tokens that are used to pay for expenses, buy, sell, and raise crowdfunding in the New World platform to help make the ecosystem more diverse.

Exchanging currencies on the New World Exchange

New World Exchange facilitates ways to cash in and cash out to fiat currencies or stable coins using service partners. This is handy for users who do not want to be exposed to the volatility of cryptocurrencies.
