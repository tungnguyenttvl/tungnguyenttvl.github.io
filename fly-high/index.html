What is Fly High ?

Fly High is a music entertainment social media platform powered by BlockChain. Here all music lovers around the world, regardless of age, experience, race or creed can provide exclusive international music entertainment content, networks, events, contests, prizes, products and services.

Features

Fly High is built on a completely new business model, making a significant contribution to the development of the entire world music economy.

Unified Information

Fly High is a decentralized, peer-to-peer database for sharing information between applications and users. Decentralized and tamper-proof data and content addressing, allowing independent collaboration without a central control center.

Value Unlock

By using cryptography, all data in Fly High is always connected to the author's identity, providing a channel for allocation, analysis, and live streaming value.

Explosive innovation

Shared, open data enables developers, creators, and organizations to innovate the way we share, use, and experience media.

How Fly High Works ?

Fly High is built on an entirely new framework and business model that can make a significant contribution to the growth of the entire music economy when applied to its own music platform.
Four basic principles were used in the design of Fly High to serve as the control point to which all concepts and ideas must adhere.

All people who actively contribute to the success of a venture should be rewarded in direct proportion to the measurable value of their contribution.

All people who create artistic content should be rewarded in direct proportion to the measurable value that content provides those who consume it.

All people who give their time in helping high quality content find an audience should be rewarded in direct proportion to the earnings of the content creators.

The cryptocurrency platform should be built on a real, viable and scalable business model.

Fly High Mission

To empower a fair, sustainable and vibrant music industry ecosystem involving all online music interaction services

To unlock the huge potential for creators and their music related metadata so an entirely new commercial marketplace may flourish

To ensure all involved are paid and acknowledged fully

To see that commercial, ethical and technical standards are set to exponentially increase innovation for the music services of the future

To connect the dots with all those involved in this shift from our current outdated music industry models, exploring new technological solutions to enliven and positively impact the music ecosystem.

Building symbiosis into music production, social media, digital and mobile, consumer consumption and distribution.
